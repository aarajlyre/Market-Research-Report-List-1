<p><ul><li>Filter Adsorption</li><li>Electrostatic Precipitator</li><li>Ozone Sterilization</li><li>Hypochlorous Acid Disinfection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air disinfection and purification machine market consists of several technologies. Filter adsorption utilizes activated carbon or HEPA filters to trap pollutants and allergens. Electrostatic precipitators charge particles, collecting them on oppositely charged plates, effectively removing smoke and dust. Ozone sterilization employs ozone gas to eliminate microorganisms, while hypochlorous acid disinfection uses a mild chlorine solution to neutralize pathogens. Each method serves distinct applications, enhancing indoor air quality and promoting health in various environments.</p></p>
