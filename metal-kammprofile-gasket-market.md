<p><p>The Metal Kammprofile Gasket market is categorized into two main types: Fixed Guide Ring Kammprofile and Floating Guide Ring Kammprofile. The Fixed Guide Ring Kammprofile features a rigid frame that secures the gasket in place, enhancing stability and reducing the risk of movement under pressure. In contrast, the Floating Guide Ring Kammprofile allows for some degree of freedom, accommodating thermal expansion and vibration, making it suitable for dynamic applications. Both types are used for high-pressure and high-temperature sealing in various industries.</p></p>
