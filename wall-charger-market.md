<p><p>The wall charger market is categorized into three main types: single-port chargers, multi-port chargers, and wireless chargers. Single-port chargers are designed to power one device at a time, offering simplicity and efficiency. Multi-port chargers accommodate multiple devices simultaneously, making them ideal for families or tech-heavy individuals. Wireless chargers eliminate the need for cables, providing a convenient, clutter-free charging experience, often using Qi technology. Each type addresses different user needs while enhancing device charging efficiency and flexibility.</p></p>
