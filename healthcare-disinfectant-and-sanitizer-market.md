<p><p>The healthcare disinfectant and sanitizer market is divided into two main types: disinfectants and sanitizers. Disinfectants are chemical agents designed to eliminate a broad spectrum of pathogens, including bacteria, viruses, and fungi, on surfaces and equipment. They typically require longer contact time to be effective. Sanitizers, on the other hand, reduce the number of germs to acceptable levels but do not eliminate all pathogens. They are commonly used for surfaces that come into direct contact with food or skin, offering more immediate protection.</p></p>
