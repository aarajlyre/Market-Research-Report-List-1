<p><ul><li>Cone Mill</li><li>Hammer Mill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical milling equipment market includes various types of machinery, with cone mills and hammer mills being prominent. Cone mills utilize a conical screen to achieve uniform particle sizes, ensuring a gentle reduction suitable for delicate materials. They are often used for granulation and deagglomeration. Hammer mills, on the other hand, employ high-speed rotating blades to fragment particles, making them ideal for heavy-duty milling of hard substances. Both types cater to the diverse needs of pharmaceutical production, emphasizing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
