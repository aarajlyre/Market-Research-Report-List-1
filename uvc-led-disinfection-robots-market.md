In contrast, UVC-LED Disinfection employs semiconductor technology to generate UVC light, presenting advantages such as longer lifespans, lower energy consumption, and immediate on/off capabilities. Both types are increasingly used in various settings, including healthcare, public transportation, and commercial spaces, to ensure effective sanitization.</p></p>
