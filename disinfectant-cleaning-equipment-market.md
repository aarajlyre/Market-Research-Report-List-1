<p><p>The disinfectant cleaning equipment market is divided into two main types: chemical methods and physical methods. Chemical methods involve the use of various disinfectant solutions, such as alcohols, bleach, and quaternary ammonium compounds, to kill pathogens on surfaces. These products are typically applied with sprayers or mops. Physical methods include techniques such as steam cleaning, UV light sanitization, and electrostatic spraying, which utilize temperature, radiation, or charged particles to eliminate microorganisms without relying on chemical agents.</p></p>
