<p><p>Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are specialized equipment utilized for excavating tunnels in various ground conditions. The market is primarily segmented into Soft Ground TBMs and Hard Rock TBMs. Soft Ground TBMs are designed for softer, cohesive soils, employing mechanisms like slurry and earth pressure balance to manage stability during excavation. In contrast, Hard Rock TBMs are built to break through tough geological formations, utilizing rotating cutting wheels and powerful thrust systems. Both types are critical in infrastructure development and urban tunneling projects.</p></p>
