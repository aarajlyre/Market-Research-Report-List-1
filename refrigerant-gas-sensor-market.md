<p><strong>The Refrigerant Gas Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochemical</li><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Pellistor</li><li>Infrared Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerant gas sensor market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Electrochemical Sensors** detect gas concentrations by measuring current changes in a chemical reaction.</p><p>2. **Semiconductor Sensors** utilize conductive materials that change resistance based on gas presence, offering sensitivity and compactness.</p><p>3. **Pellistor Sensors** consist of heated elements that react to gas exposure, causing resistance changes based on catalytic combustion.</p><p>4. **Infrared Sensors** measure gas concentrations through the absorption of infrared light, providing non-contact detection and accurate readings. </p><p>Each type serves specific applications in monitoring refrigerant leakage and ensuring safety.</p></p>
