<p><p>Image Guided Radiation Therapy (IGRT) is an advanced technique in radiation therapy that enhances the precision and accuracy of cancer treatment. By utilizing imaging technologies, such as CT, MRI, or ultrasound, IGRT allows clinicians to visualize tumors before and during treatment, ensuring that radiation is delivered precisely to the target while minimizing exposure to surrounding healthy tissues. This is particularly crucial for tumors that may move due to patient movement or internal organ function.</p><p>The IGRT market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing incidence of cancer globally, advancements in imaging technologies, and rising demand for personalized medicine.
