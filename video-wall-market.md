<p><p>The video wall market consists of three main types: LCD, LED, and DLP. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) video walls offer excellent color accuracy and are suitable for indoor applications. LED (Light Emitting Diode) video walls provide vibrant colors and high brightness, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor displays. DLP (Digital Light Processing) technology utilizes micro-mirrors for high contrast and reliability, often used in large-scale projections. Each type serves different needs depending on visibility, environment, and application.</p></p>
