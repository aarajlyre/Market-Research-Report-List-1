<p><p>The microSD card market is categorized into three main types based on storage capacity. Standard SD cards range from 8MB to 2GB, suitable for basic tasks. SDHC (Secure Digital High Capacity) cards support capacities from 2GB to 32GB, catering to moderate data needs, such as photography and music. SDXC (Secure Digital Extended Capacity) cards offer larger storage options, from 32GB up to 400GB, ideal for high-resolution video recording and extensive data storage, catering to advanced users and devices.</p></p>
