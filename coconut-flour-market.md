<p><p>The coconut flour market is segmented into three main types: whole full-fat flour, low-fat high-fiber flour, and medium-fat flour. Whole full-fat flour retains most of the coconut's fat, offering rich flavor and moisture, ideal for baking. Low-fat high-fiber flour contains reduced fat content while maximizing dietary fiber, making it suitable for health-conscious consumers. Medium-fat flour strikes a balance, providing moderate fat and fiber levels, appealing to those seeking nutritious options without compromising on taste or texture.</p></p>
