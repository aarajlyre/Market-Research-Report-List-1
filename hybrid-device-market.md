<p><p>The hybrid device market is divided into two main types: detachable hybrid devices and convertible hybrid devices. Detachable hybrid devices feature a screen that can be easily separated from the keyboard, allowing users to switch between laptop and tablet modes. In contrast, convertible hybrid devices are designed with a 360-degree hinge, enabling them to transform seamlessly between laptop and tablet configurations without detaching any components. Both types offer flexibility and versatility, catering to diverse user needs for productivity and leisure.</p></p>
