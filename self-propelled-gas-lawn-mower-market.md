<p><p>The self-propelled gas lawn mower market features two main engine types: 2-stroke and 4-stroke. 2-stroke engines are lightweight and have fewer moving parts, allowing for a more compact design. They typically provide more power for their size but require a fuel-oil mixture. In contrast, 4-stroke engines are more fuel-efficient, quieter, and environmentally friendly, running on straight gasoline. They offer better longevity and performance, making them a popular choice for residential and commercial applications, despite being slightly heavier.</p></p>
