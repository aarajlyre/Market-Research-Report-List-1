<p><p>The PTFE films market features various types, including hydrophobic and hydrophilic films. Hydrophobic PTFE films repel water, making them ideal for applications requiring moisture resistance, such as electrical insulation and protective coatings. Conversely, hydrophilic PTFE films attract water and enhance wettability, suitable for filtration and medical applications. Additionally, the "others" category encompasses specialized or niche PTFE films that may combine unique properties for specific industrial uses, contributing to the versatility and expanding applications of PTFE materials.</p></p>
