<p><ul><li>Colloidal Silica Polishing Slurry</li><li>Alumina Polishing Slurry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaN CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) slurry market is categorized primarily into two types: colloidal silica polishing slurry and alumina polishing slurry. Colloidal silica polishing slurry offers excellent surface finish and is widely used for achieving smooth and defect-free surfaces in GaN substrates. On the other hand, alumina polishing slurry provides higher removal rates and is effective in preparing surfaces with significant material removal needs. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing the performance and quality of GaN-based devices.</p></p>
