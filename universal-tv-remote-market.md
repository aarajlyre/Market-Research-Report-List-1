<p><ul><li>Infrared Remote</li><li>Bluetooth Remote</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Universal TV Remote market comprises Infrared and Bluetooth Remote categories, each serving distinct user needs. Infrared Remotes utilize line-of-sight technology, transmitting signals through infrared light, making them simple and cost-effective. However, their range is limited to direct visibility. In contrast, Bluetooth Remotes offer greater flexibility with wireless connectivity, enabling control from various angles and distances without the need for direct line-of-sight. This versatility and convenience have led to increased adoption of Bluetooth technology in universal remotes.</p></p>
