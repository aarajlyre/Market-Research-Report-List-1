<p><p>The Polyurethane Waterproof Breathable Textile market features three main types: densely woven, coated, and membrane textiles. Densely woven textiles are designed with tightly interlaced fibers to block water while allowing moisture vapor to escape. Coated textiles involve a polyurethane layer applied to fabric, enhancing waterproof capabilities without sacrificing breathability. Membrane textiles incorporate a thin polyurethane film sandwiched between fabrics, providing superior protection against moisture while maintaining ventilation. These types cater to various applications, including outdoor gear and protective apparel.</p></p>
