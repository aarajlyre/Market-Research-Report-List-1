<p><p>The trailer locks market primarily features two types: latch locks and surround locks. Latch locks are designed to secure trailer doors with a simple, easy-to-use mechanism, providing reliable security for light to medium-duty applications. In contrast, surround locks offer a higher level of protection, encasing the trailer hitch and providing a robust defense against theft. Both types cater to varying security needs, with latch locks being suitable for frequent access and surround locks ideal for enhanced safety during long-term storage.</p></p>
