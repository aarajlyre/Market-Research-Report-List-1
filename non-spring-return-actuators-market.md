<p><p>Non-spring return actuators are crucial in various industrial applications, primarily categorized into floating control and proportional control types. Floating control actuators operate in a simple on/off manner, adjusting valve positions to predetermined levels without continuous modulation, ideal for straightforward applications. In contrast, proportional control actuators provide variable control, allowing for precise positioning and continuous adjustment of valve openings, enhancing system efficiency and responsiveness. Both types cater to specific operational needs, balancing complexity and performance based on application requirements.</p></p>
