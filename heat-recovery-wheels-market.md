<p><strong>The Heat Recovery Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat recovery wheels market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Condensation Rotors** capture and transfer heat by condensing moisture from exhaust air, enhancing energy efficiency in HVAC systems. </p><p>2. **Hygroscopic Rotors** utilize materials that absorb and release moisture to recover latent heat, making them ideal for humid environments.</p><p>3. **Sorption Rotors** leverage chemical processes to extract heat and moisture, offering improved performance in diverse climatic conditions. </p><p>Each type enhances energy efficiency and indoor air quality in various applications.</p></p>
