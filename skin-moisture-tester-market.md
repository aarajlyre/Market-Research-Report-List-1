<p><ul><li>Resistive Tester</li><li>Capacitive Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin moisture tester market includes two primary types: resistive and capacitive testers. Resistive testers measure skin hydration by assessing the electrical resistance between two electrodes; lower resistance indicates higher moisture content. Capacitive testers, on the other hand, gauge skin moisture by evaluating changes in capacitance in response to the skin's dielectric properties. Both types provide valuable insights for skincare professionals and consumers, helping to tailor hydration treatments and monitor skin health effectively.</p></p>
