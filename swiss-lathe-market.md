<p><p>The Swiss lathe market comprises two main types: CNC and conventional lathes. CNC Swiss lathes utilize computer numerical control technology for enhanced precision and automation, allowing for complex part production with minimal manual intervention. In contrast, conventional Swiss lathes operate through manual controls, offering flexibility but requiring skilled operators for setup and adjustments. Both types serve important roles in industries like aerospace and medical, with CNC machines favoring high-volume, intricate tasks, while conventional lathes are suited for simpler, bespoke jobs.</p></p>
