<p><p>Melt screen changers are essential in processing industries to improve polymer quality by removing impurities. The market is categorized into continuous, semi-continuous, and discontinuous types. Continuous screen changers operate without interruption, ensuring a steady flow of material. Semi-continuous changers alternate between filtration and processing, while discontinuous changers require a complete stop for maintenance or screen replacement. Other types may include automated systems or specialized models for niche applications, catering to diverse industry needs and processing speeds.</p></p>
