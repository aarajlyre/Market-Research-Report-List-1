<p><p>The Natural Dissolvable Sutures market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on Catgut Plain Sutures and Catgut Chromic Sutures. Catgut Plain Sutures are made from purified collagen, offering quick absorption and minimal tissue reaction, making them suitable for fast-healing tissues. In contrast, Catgut Chromic Sutures undergo a chromium salt treatment to slow absorption, providing extended support during healing. Both types are biodegradable, making them environmentally friendly options for surgical procedures or wound closures, catering to the preferences of healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
