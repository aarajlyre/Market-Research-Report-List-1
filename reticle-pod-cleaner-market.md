<p><p>The Reticle POD Cleaner market encompasses two primary types: EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) POD Cleaners and Non-EUV POD Cleaners. EUV POD Cleaners are designed specifically for cleaning reticles used in advanced semiconductor manufacturing that employs EUV lithography, ensuring the utmost cleanliness to maintain precision in chip production. Non-EUV POD Cleaners, on the other hand, cater to traditional photolithography processes and are utilized for various applications across the semiconductor industry, focusing on maintaining reticle integrity and performance amid diverse cleaning challenges.</p></p>
