<p><p>The articulated telehandler market comprises three main types: compact telehandlers, high reach telehandlers, and heavy lift telehandlers. Compact telehandlers are designed for confined spaces, offering maneuverability and versatility for light to medium loads. High reach telehandlers provide extended lift heights, making them ideal for construction and maintenance tasks where access to elevated areas is necessary. Heavy lift telehandlers are built for lifting substantial weights, catering to industries that require robust machines for heavy-duty operations. Each type serves specific operational needs.</p></p>
