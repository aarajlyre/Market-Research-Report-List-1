<p><strong>The Hard Mask Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic Hard Mask Material</li><li>Inorganic Hard Mask Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hard mask material market is primarily divided into two types: organic and inorganic hard mask materials. Organic hard masks, often based on polymers, provide excellent selectivity and pattern fidelity during etching processes in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, inorganic hard masks, typically made from materials like silicon nitride or silicon dioxide, offer superior thermal stability and chemical resistance. Both types are essential in the fabrication of microelectronic devices, enabling complex layer structures while protecting underlying materials during processing.</p></p>
