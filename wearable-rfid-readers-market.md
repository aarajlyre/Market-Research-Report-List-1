<p><strong>The Wearable RFID Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID System</li><li>HF RFID System</li><li>UHF RFID System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wearable RFID readers market comprises several types based on operating frequencies. LF (Low Frequency) RFID systems, operating between 125-134 kHz, are ideal for short-range applications like animal tracking. HF (High Frequency) RFID systems, operating at 13.56 MHz, are commonly used for access control and payment systems, offering moderate range and speed. UHF (Ultra-High Frequency) RFID systems, from 860-960 MHz, provide longer read ranges and faster data rates, suitable for inventory management. Additionally, "Others" include specialized systems catering to niche applications.</p></p>
