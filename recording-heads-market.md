<p><ul><li>Magnetoresistive Head</li><li>Giant Magnetoresistive Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recording heads market comprises various types of technologies used in data storage devices. Magnetoresistive heads utilize changes in electrical resistance to read data, offering improved performance and sensitivity over traditional inductive heads. Giant Magnetoresistive (GMR) heads take this further by incorporating multilayer magnetic structures, significantly enhancing data storage density and read/write speeds. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for high-capacity storage solutions in hard drives and other media, reflecting advancements in data management and retrieval efficiency.</p></p>
