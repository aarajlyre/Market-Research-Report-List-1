<p><ul><li>Core Type Traction Transformer</li><li>Shell Type Traction Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The traction transformer market encompasses two main types: core type and shell type. Core type traction transformers feature a magnetic core that facilitates efficient energy transfer and is often used in lightweight applications. In contrast, shell type traction transformers have a more enclosed structure, providing better mechanical stability and lower losses, making them suitable for high-performance systems. Both types play critical roles in electrified transportation systems, ensuring reliable power supply for trains and trams.</p></p>
