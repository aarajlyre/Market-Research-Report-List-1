<p><p>The grain shelling machine market comprises two main types: vertical type shelling machines and horizontal type shelling machines. Vertical shelling machines utilize a vertical feeding mechanism, allowing for efficient operation and reduced space requirements, making them ideal for smaller setups. In contrast, horizontal shelling machines facilitate larger processing capacities and are often preferred for industrial applications where high throughput is essential. Both types serve to effectively remove outer shells from grains, optimizing productivity and enhancing product quality in grain processing.</p></p>
