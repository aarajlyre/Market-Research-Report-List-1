<p><p>Cloud-based restaurant software encompasses three main market types: SaaS, PaaS, and IaaS. SaaS (Software as a Service) provides ready-to-use applications for restaurant management, enabling easy access and updates over the internet. PaaS (Platform as a Service) offers a framework for developers to build custom applications tailored to restaurant needs, facilitating integration and scalability. IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service) delivers virtualized computing resources, allowing restaurants to manage their own infrastructure while benefiting from flexibility and cost-efficiency in deployment.</p></p>
