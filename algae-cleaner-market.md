<p><p>The algae cleaner market is categorized into oxidation and non-oxidation types. Oxidation-type cleaners utilize chemical agents, such as chlorine or ozone, to effectively eliminate algae by breaking down their cellular structure. In contrast, non-oxidation type cleaners use alternative methods, such as biological catalysts or physical removal, to control algae growth without chemical reactions. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to different applications in pools, water systems, and aquaculture, while addressing environmental concerns and efficiency in algae management.</p></p>
