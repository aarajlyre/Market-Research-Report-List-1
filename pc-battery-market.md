<p><p>The PC battery market consists of three primary types: Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd), Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH), and Lithium batteries. NiCd batteries are known for their robustness and performance in extreme conditions but suffer from memory effect. NiMH batteries offer enhanced capacity and are more environmentally friendly than NiCd, making them popular in portable devices. Lithium batteries dominate the market due to their high energy density, lightweight, and longer cycle life, making them ideal for modern laptops and portable devices.</p></p>
