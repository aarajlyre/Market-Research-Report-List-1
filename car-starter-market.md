<p><strong>The Car Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial (Sliding Armature)</li><li>Coaxial (Sliding Gear)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car starter market features two primary types: Axial (Sliding Armature) and Coaxial (Sliding Gear). The Axial type utilizes a sliding armature that directly engages with the flywheel, delivering torque through a linear motion. In contrast, the Coaxial system employs a sliding gear mechanism, allowing for a more compact design where the gear aligns with the flywheel for efficient power transfer. Both systems are critical for reliable engine starts, with varying applications in automotive engineering.</p></p>
