<p><p>The pneumatic equipment market is categorized primarily into three types: straight nozzles, angled nozzles, and others. Straight nozzles provide a direct flow of air, ideal for applications requiring precise airflow without obstruction. Angled nozzles offer versatility in directing air in hard-to-reach areas, enhancing efficiency in various tasks. The "others" category encompasses specialized nozzles designed for specific functions, such as diffusing or atomizing air. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial needs, optimizing performance and productivity in different environments.</p></p>
