<p><p>The insulin delivery system market comprises various types, including insulin syringes, insulin pumps, and insulin pens. Insulin syringes are traditional devices that allow for manual injection of insulin, offering flexibility but requiring skills in dosage calculation. Insulin pumps deliver continuous subcutaneous insulin via a programmable device, providing better glucose control and convenience. Insulin pens are user-friendly devices that come pre-filled or refillable, simplifying the injection process with preset dosages and eliminating the need for separate syringes, enhancing adherence and comfort.</p></p>
