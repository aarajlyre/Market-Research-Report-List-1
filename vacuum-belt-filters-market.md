<p><p>The Vacuum Belt Filters market can be categorized into three main types: MTF (Medium Temperature Filtration), HTH (High Temperature Filtration), and HRB (High Resistance Belt) filters. MTF type filters are designed for moderate temperature applications, offering efficient dewatering capabilities. HTH type filters operate effectively in high-temperature environments, catering to specific industrial needs. HRB type filters are engineered for enhanced durability and resistance, making them suitable for challenging filtrations involving abrasive materials. Each type addresses unique operational requirements in various industries.</p></p>
