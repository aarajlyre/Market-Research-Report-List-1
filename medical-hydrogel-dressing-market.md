<p><p>The medical hydrogel dressing market includes three primary types: Amorphous Hydrogel Dressings, which are gel-like and maintain a moist environment for wound healing; Impregnated Gauze, which combines gauze with hydrogel to provide moisture and protection while being easy to apply; and Hydrogel Sheets, which offer a thin, flexible layer of hydrogel that adheres to the skin, providing effective moisture retention and reduced pain during dressing changes. Each type serves to enhance wound healing and patient comfort.</p></p>
