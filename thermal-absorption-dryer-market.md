<p><p>The thermal absorption dryer market is divided into two main types: intermittent and continuous drying systems. Intermittent dryers operate in batch modes, allowing for the treatment of various material quantities, making them suitable for smaller production needs or varying material types. In contrast, continuous dryers maintain a constant flow of materials, ensuring consistent drying efficiency and higher output, ideal for large-scale operations. Both types cater to different industrial requirements and processes, enhancing overall thermal drying efficiency.</p></p>
